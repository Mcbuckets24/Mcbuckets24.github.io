If you ever find me in the wild, it's almost a guarantee I'll have my headphones on. Music is the background to quite literally everything I do. I think music expresses the human experience better than anything else. Whether it's the greatest or worst day of your life, there's a song that can make you feel understood. That's why I love it. Interests, for me, lie all over the place like any other music fan, but I think I can boil it down to something like this: Hip Hop, Midwest Emo, Indie J-Rock, Psychedelia, Jazz (fusion), Soul, and anything that my brain happens to enjoy. My favorite artists are Fishmans, Kendrick Lamar, and Harrison Gordon. My favorite album is 98.12.28 Otokutachi no Wakare by Fishmans. I'm always open to recommendations and honestly feel like I've barely scratched the surface of the music scene, so I'd love to chat about whoever your favorite artist might be!
