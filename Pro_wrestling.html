Pro wrestling is Earth's greatest art form. Admittedly, I've only been a fan since 2020 and my knowledge is far from comprehensive, but I like to think that I know my way around the space to an extent. I'd say my current focus lies in modern AEW and NJPW, 90s AJPW, and 00s ROH & NOAH. Product outside of those are things I'm well aware of, but uncomfortable calling myself any kind of expert on. I'm always working on some project surrounding wrestling, but have been really busy with personal stuff lately, so I'm not sure when the next one will drop. I have a good amount of stuff already made, so feel free to check them out and see what you think! Overall, my favorite wrestlers ever probably go something like: Bryan Danielson, Kenta Kobashi, and Jon Moxley. My favorite match is Kenta Kobashi vs. Samoa Joe 10/01/05. I really believe that there's nothing more special than pro wrestling, there's something for everyone in the space, so go find what gives you life!
